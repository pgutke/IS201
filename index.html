Palmer Gutke

Decisions Determine Destiny
-Thomas S Monson

I'm a firm believer our course in life is a compilation of thousands upon thousands up of minute decisions, not only ours, but of those that came before us. Because of this, we have the responsibility to direct our path to where those who come after us have the best starting point possible.

Looking Back

I attribute any success I've had in life to how my parents raised me. They put me and my siblings first, in a world that says that selfishness is the way to get ahead. Through their example, they taught us the value of hard work, integrity, honesty, selflessness, kindness and wholesome fun.

Moving Forward

With the foundation of character that my parents gave me, I hope to make a diffence in the lives of others. Good is best accomplished through collaboration. One of the greatest examples of this in my life is my wife Kaywen. Through her, came to know that open and honest communication is the first essential step to accomplishing meaningful and impactful goals.
