About Me

Pre-Business Student At BYU

Howdy!

I'm Palmer Gutke and I love life! I believe that hard work, combined with wholesome fun are essential to finding joy in life. Some ways I find this joy is through serving others, going on adventures in the outdoors, cooking, working on cars, improving my Spanish, and getting to know people and their story.

One of the greatest experiences I ever had was serving as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Panama as well as upstate New York. It changed me completely to experience completely different cultures, and I learned to love the people relate to them. (Here's a link to learn more about what I did as a missionary: "Who Are The Missionaries?" ) After finishing my service as a full-time missionary, I realized that I wanted to spend the rest of my life changing people's lives for the better and helping them find the good in the world.

Since then, I discovered the Experience Design and Management major here at BYU, which will give me the tools I need to continue to make a positive impact on the world, one person at a time. I also met the love of life, Kaywen Tipa, and we were married April 23, 2022.
